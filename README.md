This is software that allows you to track and organise everything you copy onto your clipboard instantly and is accessible on all your devices including android or ios.
Some of the features include:Quick search  by category, content, web address, application and much more, Icloud sync which enables the copied snippets
to be saved and sync across all your devices and Complete History which can help tou retrieve any snippets from the first time you used the app.
It is accessible anywhere across all your devices even on the GO!
